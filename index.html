Our Approach

From Commitment to Carbon Zero

Ameresco doesn't hand you a report and walk away. We own every stage of the journey — from the first audit to decades of operation — ensuring accountability, performance and value at each step. One partner. Total responsibility.

Step 01

Assess

Deep-dive energy audits, site surveys and baseline carbon analysis. We quantify every opportunity before committing a penny of yours or ours.

Step 02

Design

Full engineering design, technology selection and financial modelling — bespoke to your objectives, estate profile and long-term budget.

Step 03

Finance

ESCO contracts, off-balance-sheet structures, grant navigation and blended finance. Capital barriers removed — the deal works for your organisation.

Step 04

Build

Turnkey EPC delivery — project management, supply chain, grid connections and commissioning, all handled end-to-end without passing the baton.

Step 05

Operate

Long-term O&M, 24/7 remote monitoring and guaranteed performance. We stay invested in outcomes — not just outputs — for the life of the contract.

Step 06

Optimise

Continuous energy analytics, demand management and technology refresh. Your assets improve over time — not just maintained, but made better.
